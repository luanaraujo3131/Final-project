IMC Calculator

The IMC (BMI) Calculator shows whether your weight is in a healthy range for your height. It’s a quick and simple tool to check your overall weight status.

BMR and Caloric expenditure

The BMR Calculator estimates how many calories your body burns at rest, while the caloric expenditure value shows your total daily energy needs based on activity level. It helps you understand how much you should eat to maintain, lose, or gain weight.
